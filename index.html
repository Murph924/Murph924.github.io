The Neverlasting is Daniel Murphy’s current musical project. Blending harsh experimental post-rock Sounds with atmospheric and electronic soundscapes, The Neverlasting aspires to make music that feels new and true to itself and its messaging.

Written and Produced entirely by Daniel Murphy.
